# 💸 Fund Tournaments

Fans can **support the community** by funding tournaments. Funding helps increase prize pools and can earn you rewards based on the tournament’s success.

---

## 📝 How to Fund

1. Browse tournaments on Nexra’s web app.
2. Select a tournament you want to support.
3. Contribute to the prize pool directly through the platform.
4. Track your contributions and any rewards earned.

---

## 💡 Tips for Fans

- Funding tournaments with more participants can increase potential rewards.
- Support your favorite gamers to influence the competition.
- Keep track of your funded tournaments and any rewards on your dashboard.
