# 🪙 Rewards

Nexra handles all **prize pools, entry fees, and payouts automatically**, so you can focus on competing and having fun.

---

## 💰 How Rewards Work

- Entry fees are collected automatically when joining a tournament.  
- Prize pools grow with every participant.  
- Winners receive their rewards automatically at the end of the tournament.  
- Tips from fans are added directly to your account.

---

## 🏆 Viewing Your Rewards

- Check your **dashboard** to see your current and past winnings.  
- View completed tournaments and the rewards you earned.  
- Keep track of any tips received from fans supporting you.

---

## ✅ Tips for Gamers

- Focus on tournaments that match your skill level to maximize your chances.  
- Track your performance across tournaments to improve.  
- Encourage friends to participate in tournaments you join or host to grow the prize pool.
